How does Agile SDLC work?

  • Projects are broken into small cycles called sprints.
  • Each sprint usually lasts 1 to 4 weeks.
  • Teams plan what to do in each sprint in a meeting called a sprint planning session.
  • Daily stand-ups are short meetings where team members discuss progress.
  • At the end of a sprint, teams review what they have done in a sprint review meeting.
  • After the review, there is a meeting to discuss what went well and what can be improved.
