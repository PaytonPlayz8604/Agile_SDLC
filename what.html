What is Agile SDLC?

  • The Agile Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC) is an iterative and incremental software development methodology that prioritizes flexibility, collaboration, and customer feedback.
  • It focuses on working together and being flexible.
  • This approach allows for continuous improvement and quick responses to changes.
  • Agile is different from traditional methods that follow a strict sequence of steps.
